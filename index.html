BRIEF ABOUT FAKE NEWS

What is fake news?

Fake news defined by New York Times as a made-up story with an intention to deceive,often for a secondary gain,is arguably one of the most serious challenges facing the news industry today.Fake news or junk news or pseudo-news is a type of yellow journalism or propaganda that consists of deliberate disinformation or hoaxes spread via traditional print and broadcast news media or online social media.


OUR GOAL:

The goal of the Fake News Detection System is to explore different algorithm or combination of algorithms to mitigate fake news problems.We believe thaat these algorithms hold promises for significantly automating parts of the procedure human fact checkers use today to determine if a story is real or a hoax.


Process:

Fortunately,the process can be broken down into steps or stages.A helpful first step towards identifying fake news is to understand what other news organizations are saying about the topic.We believe automating this process could serve a useful building block in a machine learning fact-checking pipeline.
